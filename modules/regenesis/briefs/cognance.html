Creative integration
Cognance is used here as a “flexibility amplifier”: not brute-force stimulation, but a cleaner perception + association mode to support integration, creative throughput, and social fluidity.

Why it belongs in Neuro-Dynamics

  • Supports lateral association and creative problem solving.
  • Helps attention + memory encoding (integration days).
  • Improves narrative integration after heavy interventions.

Neuro-dynamics relevance

  • Raises cognitive flexibility and lateral association for creative problem solving.
  • Supports attention + memory encoding (M1 / cholinergic lane).
  • Pairs well with “integration days” (clarity without heavy disruption).
  • Improves narrative integration after heavy interventions.

Best used as

  • A “daily cognition geometry” tool (clarity, pattern-linking, flow).
  • Not a replacement for sleep or baseline regulation.
