02. THE NIGHT PROTOCOL

STEP 01

DOWNSHIFT

  • Reduce brightness and screen exposure 60 to 120 minutes before bed.
  • Lower cognitive load: no conflict, no intense planning, no doom scrolling.
  • Make the room “boring”: dim, quiet, cool.
STEP 02

TEMPERATURE + BREATH

  • Cool environment supports sleep onset and continuity.
  • Slow nasal breathing signals safety and reduces sympathetic tone.
  • Keep it simple: your goal is a body that believes it can let go.
STEP 03

NUTRIENT TIMING

  • Avoid heavy meals too close to sleep if they raise temperature or heart rate.
  • Prioritize steady blood sugar and no late stimulants.
  • Consistency matters more than perfection.
STEP 04

STACKS ARE SUPPORT, NOT THE ENGINE

  • Supplements serve sleep quality, not the other way around.
  • If a compound increases arousal, it is not “regenesis” for that night.
  • We privilege what increases depth, continuity, and morning clarity.
