THE ARCHITECTURE

PRIMAL STRUCTURE // THE 5 PRIMITIVES

SUBSTRATE PRIMACY

The framework posits a single state space, the Substrate (S), as the sole fundamental arena. Spacetime is treated not as a pre-existing container, but as a stabilized presentation of substrate configurations governed by a closed update cycle.

THE ARENA

SUBSTRATE (S)

$$ \mathcal{X} \equiv S $$

The sole fundamental domain of states. All structure, regularity, and appearance occur as patterns within S. It is the raw configuration space prior to restriction.

THE GENERATOR

GLOBAL DYNAMICS (H_tot)

$$ H_{tot}: S \rightarrow S $$

The global dynamical principle acting on S. It acts as the organizer of substrate evolution and the anchor of global coherence, independent of local observation.

ACCESSIBILITY

PROJECTION / COHERENCE (Λ)

$$ \Lambda: S \rightarrow S_{loc} $$

The coherence operator. It restricts full global complexity to stable, contextual structure. Locality and classical persistence arise as stability under this projection.

EFFECTIVE DESCRIPTION

EMERGENCE (Ω)

$$ \Omega: S_{loc} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}_{eff} $$

The induced rule describing accessible dynamics. Spacetime, fields, and histories are strictly treated as effective descriptions of the stable sector, not fundamental entities.

UPDATE RULES

$$ H_{tot} \rightarrow S \rightarrow \Lambda \rightarrow \Omega \rightarrow \Delta \rightarrow H_{tot} $$
01. GLOBAL EVOLUTION H_tot drives substrate transformation.
02. RESTRICTION Λ yields a stable, contextual presentation of the evolving substrate.
03. EFFECTIVE DYNAMICS Ω summarizes the accessible dynamics of that presentation.
04. INTEGRATION Δ incorporates emergent regularities and feeds them back as stabilizing constraints.
THE CLOSURE

RECURSIVE INTEGRATION (Δ)

The closure rule. It integrates emergent regularities and feeds them back into the conditions that stabilize global organization. This ensures that reality is not a static ontology but a self-maintaining cycle of transformation.
