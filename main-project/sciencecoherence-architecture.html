03 — Global Pages

Shared Infrastructure

Five pages that operate across all three branches, forming the connective tissue of the platform.

Entry Point

Home

The portal. Introduces Science Coherence as a unified field, presents the three branches visually, and routes each visitor toward their natural entry point. Contains a strong above-the-fold statement of purpose and a secondary CTA toward retreats or community.

  • Hero: Core philosophy statement
  • Branch selector (visual, felt)
  • Featured content from each branch
  • Retreat teaser + community CTA
Foundation

About / Manifesto

The intellectual and personal origin story. Who is behind this work, why it exists, and what the unified model asserts. This page builds trust with all three audience types simultaneously — personal for the seeker, rigorous for the researcher, credible for the optimizer.

  • Founder story & credentials
  • The Science Coherence model
  • What makes this different
  • The invitation to participate
Core Offering

Retreats

The physical destination. Describes the retreat center concept, the programs offered (aligned to each branch), the experience, and how to apply. This is the highest-value conversion point on the platform.

  • Retreat center vision & location
  • Program tracks (per branch)
  • What to expect & prepare
  • Application / waitlist form
Platform Hub

Community

The living network. Onboards visitors into the WhatsApp AI agent channel and the broader community. Frames the community as an ongoing transmission — not a passive forum but an active, guided space.

  • WhatsApp AI: what it does, how to join
  • Community values & culture
  • Events & live sessions
  • Member spotlights
Access Point

Contact / Apply

Clean, intentional. Separates retreat applications from general inquiries. Includes press/collaboration contact. No noise — only purposeful channels.

  • Retreat application form
  • General inquiry
  • Press & collaboration
  • Social links
Optional — Phase 2

Library / Index

A unified search and discovery layer across all three branches. Allows visitors to explore content by topic, keyword, or branch. Becomes more valuable as the content library scales.

  • Cross-branch search
  • Topic clusters & tags
  • Reading pathways
  • Recommended starting points
04 — Conversion Architecture

The Funnel

From discovery to retreat — a five-stage flow that converts a curious visitor into an engaged community member and, ultimately, a retreat participant.

STAGE 01

Discovery

Visitor arrives via search, social, or referral. Lands on branch content or home page. The content is the hook — no forced opt-ins at this stage.

↓ Depth of content
STAGE 02

Capture

Visitor engages with a protocol download, manifesto PDF, or gated essay. Exchanges email for access. Enters email sequence aligned to their branch.

↓ Email + value
STAGE 03

Activation

Visitor joins the WhatsApp AI channel. Begins receiving automated guidance, weekly insights, and protocols tailored to their entry branch.

↓ WhatsApp onboard
STAGE 04

Community

Member participates in community events, live sessions, and discussions. Cross-branch exposure begins — Regenesis members discover Cosmos, Ethos draws all together.

↓ Trust + belonging
STAGE 05

Retreat

Member applies for the retreat. The physical immersion is positioned as the culmination — not an upsell, but the full embodiment of the Science Coherence framework in person.

↓ Application
05 — Strategic Notes

Design Principles

Architectural decisions that will shape both the site and the platform as it grows.

Single URL, Three Worlds

All three branches live on sciencecoherence.com with clear visual differentiation per branch — color, typography weight, tone — while sharing a unified design language. No subdomains. One platform, one identity, three doorways.

Content as Currency

The depth and originality of the content IS the marketing. Every article, protocol, and essay builds authority and trust before any commercial conversation begins. Launch with a minimum of 3 strong pieces per branch before going public.

WhatsApp as Living Transmission

The AI agent is not a chatbot — it is framed as a continuous, intelligent guidance system. This positioning elevates it from utility to philosophy: coherence delivered daily, directly into people's lives.

Retreat as Apex, Not Product

The retreat should never feel like something being sold. It is the physical destination that the entire platform points toward — the place where the three branches converge in embodied experience. Treat it as an invitation, not an offering.

Phase 1 vs Phase 2

Phase 1: Home, About, 3 Branch Homes, Retreats page (waitlist), Community page (WhatsApp onboard). Phase 2: Full protocol libraries, The Manual, Research archive, Library/Index, Events calendar. Build the container first, fill it with precision.

Cross-Branch Coherence

Every page, regardless of branch, should reference the others naturally. A protocol in Regenesis links to its theoretical basis in Cosmos. A Cosmos essay links to its practical application in Ethos. The site itself models the philosophy it teaches: everything is connected.
