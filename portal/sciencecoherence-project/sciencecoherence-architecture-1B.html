Introduces the two complementary axes of the framework: the Science of Being (what reality is — ontology, physics, mechanism) and the Science of Living (how to act within it — biology, protocol, practice). Map vs. Territory Theory ↔ Application How to Read This Framework

Introduces Purity (Substrate Integrity), Rhythm (Temporal Precision), and Resonance (Field Integration) as the three foundational laws of Coherence. Uses accessible analogies and cross-domain examples to show how the same three principles operate in biology, cognition, and physics.
Purity — Signal vs. Noise Rhythm — Timing Is Structure Resonance — Coupling With the Field How the Pillars Interconnect

Maps the three branches — Regenesis (body), Ethos (mind), Cosmos (field) — and helps the reader identify their natural entry point based on interest, background, or immediate need. Explains that branches can be explored in any order and that cross-links will surface connections naturally.
