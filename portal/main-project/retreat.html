The Location

Vilcabamba.

Longevity.

The indigenous Vilcabamba population has been studied for over a century for anomalous longevity and vitality. The valley sits at 1500m in the Andes, with a magnetic field anomaly, pristine mineral water, and a near-perfect 12/12 equatorial light cycle year-round.

Within the Science Coherence framework, Vilcabamba is not simply a beautiful place to hold a retreat. It is an environment whose substrate integrity, temporal rhythm, and electromagnetic resonance align with all three pillars — Purity, Rhythm, and Resonance — before any deliberate intervention begins.
