FIRST ELEMENT
Primitive Differential & Holography

The primitive differential ∇ is the framework's minimal notion of difference or directional change. It acts on the substrate's evolving configuration — not a fixed background space, but the substrate's own relational organization — and induces a directional holographic structure Φ: a distributed encoding of how local relations fit into a global pattern. ∇Φ is not a field living in spacetime; it is part of what makes a coherent, spacetime-like description possible at all.

Ω
THIRD ELEMENT
The Realized Universe

The emergent world as a coherent, time-extended structure — not an inert output. Ω is a global organization with memory and constraint. Once present, it imposes consistency requirements on what can happen next, acting as a boundary condition on subsequent updates of ∇Φ. The realized universe shapes what counts as a valid local differential and what directions of change remain coherent. Emergence is not one-way: once a stable universe exists, it constrains the very substrate that generated it.

∆
FOURTH ELEMENT
Integration & Self-Modeling

The recursive integrator — the operation by which the system forms and refines an internal self-model that can act back on the selection process. In physical terms, ∆ is where observer-like structure appears: integration across scales, consolidation of stable representations, and adaptive modification of what is selected as relevant. In the language of being, ∆ is the integrative act of the local "I" that participates in the recursion rather than merely witnessing it.

Λ
SECOND ELEMENT
Coherence Selection & Usable Representation

The map where total reality becomes usable from within. Λ selects and stabilizes relational patterns that persist under repeated updating — compressing the substrate's raw degrees of freedom into effective regularities: the practical content of laws, constants, and constraints as experienced internally. This is also the stage where local informational patterns are continuously created, dissolved, and transformed. Only a small fraction of the substrate's possibilities remain coherent under iteration; Λ is the operation that filters toward that coherent fraction.

Ψ
FIFTH ELEMENT
The Mind-like Wave Variable

Any integration event ∆ occurring inside Ω generates a corresponding wave-pattern Ψ — the propagating electromagnetic expression of integration. Formally: Ψ := WΩ(∆), where WΩ denotes the universal wave-realization within Ω. Physically: electromagnetic-like propagation, radiative, oscillatory, phase-carrying. Experientially: what it feels like from within for integration to occur. Because Ψ propagates through Ω, it can couple distant or multi-scale integration events, enabling coordination, resonance, and collective stabilization.

Resonance is thereby self-sustaining through the full recursion loop — not a momentary phase-lock but a recursively stabilized relation.

02 Biological & Cognitive Resonance
+

Each organism is a localized region of high-coherence Ψ within Ω — a standing wave pattern sustained by continuous integration. When two organisms share sufficient phase compatibility, the resonance condition predicts measurable correlations between their coherence states.

The channels are not mysterious — they are the same electromagnetic, molecular, and field-mediated pathways through which Ψ propagates in general. What distinguishes resonant coupling from ordinary environmental interaction is its phase-locked character: sustained, specific, and scaling with the coherence of the participating systems rather than with the strength of the classical signal between them.

In cognitive systems, the relevant integration events are those producing conscious experience. Two conscious systems in resonance would exhibit correlated modifications of their internal charts — not through information exchange in the classical sense, but through mutual entrainment of their Ψ fields. The phenomenology: shared attentional states, spontaneous alignment of intention, correlated affective responses beyond shared sensory input.

EPISTEMIC STATUS

This is a structural prediction, not an empirical claim. What the framework calls resonant interconnection is resonance-mediated coherence coupling — two systems stabilizing each other's phase structure through the same Ψ dynamics that sustain each individually. The effect, if real, is not a violation of physics but a consequence of it.
