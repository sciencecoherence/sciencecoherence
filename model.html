Reality is a self-actualizing recursion linking generative differentiation, structural articulation, realized configuration, and instantiated structure. Existence itself is a manifestation of coherence: a self-sustaining oscillatory field whose temporal periodicity constitutes the rhythm of reality.

Formal Results

Key
theorems.

The model yields precise formal conditions — each a structural consequence of the closed recursion, not an external assumption imposed upon it.

Theorem 01
Coherence as Alignment
High coherence → stability, resilience, meaningful pattern

A system is coherent when its internal differentials and external structure are mutually compatible, allowing persistence. Systems that maintain alignment across scales persist; those that lose alignment fragment or dissolve. The universe is a self-correcting quantum intelligence whose goal is the preservation and refinement of its own coherence.

Theorem 02
Temporal Synchronization
∇Ψ = 1 — the covariant coherence law

Local phase evolution aligns with the global stroboscopic cadence of the closed recursion. Spatial transport and temporal periodic reconstruction are locked to the same alignment principle. ∇Ψ = 1 is not two constraints — it is one coherence law written in the language of parallel transport.

Theorem 03
Knowing as Re-cohering
Knowing ≡ Re-cohering

Every act of perception, inference, or attention is a local correction reducing mismatch between the stabilized informational configuration and the internal chart. Knowledge is not a static description of reality but the recurring event by which reality re-aligns with itself. Cognition is a local return toward coherence equilibrium.

Theorem 04
Consciousness as Phase
Consciousness ≡ high-coherence dynamical phase of the closed recursion

Not a substance. Not an epiphenomenon. The mode the recursion enters when dual stabilization becomes sustained. Once Ψ is stationary under ∆, the system carries a stable internal chart of its own state. Self-awareness is the recursion recognizing its own configuration through internal alignment.

Theorem 05
Resolution of the One-State Paradox
Multiplicity persists because differentiation is internal to the recursion

The universe avoids collapse into a single undifferentiated state because stabilization is localized and renewed by the loop itself — not granted by external measurement. The paradox dissolves once observation is understood as an internal alignment event: a local episode of re-cohering in which the chart becomes stable enough to carry information about the state from within.
